# Privacy Policy for Prompter Fresh

**Effective Date:** [Current Date]  
**Last Updated:** [Current Date]

## Introduction

Prompter Fresh ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Prompts and Templates**: Text content you create, edit, or save within the app
- **User Preferences**: Language settings, theme preferences, and app configurations
- **Account Data**: If you choose to sync data across devices (optional)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, app version
- **Usage Analytics**: Feature usage patterns, performance metrics (anonymized)
- **Error Reports**: Crash logs and technical diagnostics (no personal data)

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- **Personal Identity**: We do not require or collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- **Location Data**: We do not access or collect your geographic location
- **Contacts**: We do not access your device contacts or calendar
- **Camera/Microphone**: We do not access camera or microphone without explicit permission

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **App Functionality**: Store your prompts and preferences locally on your device
- **Performance**: Improve app performance and fix technical issues
- **Features**: Provide personalized recommendations and enhanced user experience

### Data Storage
- **Local Storage**: All your content is stored locally on your device
- **Cloud Sync**: Optional cloud synchronization (user-controlled)
- **No Server Storage**: We do not store your prompts or personal content on our servers

## Third-Party Services

### AI Service Providers
When you use AI features, your prompts may be sent to:
- **OpenAI** (if using OpenAI models)
- **Anthropic** (if using Claude models)
- **Google** (if using Gemini models)

**Important**: These services have their own privacy policies. Your prompts are sent directly to these services and are not stored by us.

### Analytics Services
- **Google Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics (opt-out available)
- **Crashlytics**: Anonymous crash reporting for app stability

### Advertising (Free Version)
- **Google AdMob**: Displays ads in the free version
- **No Personal Data**: Ads are based on context, not personal information

## Your Privacy Rights

### Data Control
- **Local Control**: All your data is stored on your device
- **Deletion**: Uninstalling the app removes all your data
- **Export**: You can export your prompts at any time
- **Opt-Out**: You can disable analytics in app settings

### No Account Required
- **Anonymous Usage**: You can use the app without creating an account
- **No Registration**: No email or personal information required
- **Local Operation**: App works entirely offline if desired

## Data Security

### Security Measures
- **Local Encryption**: Sensitive data is encrypted on your device
- **Secure Transmission**: All network communications use HTTPS
- **No Central Database**: We don't maintain a central database of user content
- **Regular Updates**: Security patches and updates are provided regularly

### API Key Security
- **User-Controlled**: API keys are stored locally and encrypted
- **No Transmission**: We never transmit or store your API keys
- **User Responsibility**: Users are responsible for API key security

## Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

## International Users

### Data Processing
- **Local Processing**: Data is processed locally on your device
- **Cross-Border**: If you use cloud sync, data may be processed in different countries
- **GDPR Compliance**: We comply with applicable data protection laws

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes through:
- **In-App Notification**: Update announcement in the app
- **Version Notes**: Changes documented in app updates
- **Effective Date**: New effective date will be clearly displayed
